This scenario concerns truck drivers arriving at the construction site to deliver cargo.

This is a specification of the "On-site Logistics" where the cargo is delivered by trucks.

Intentionally left empty.

DefinitionsðŸ”—

pickup personðŸ”—

The person, an IfcPerson, responsible for picking up the cargo.

entry pointðŸ”—

The IfcZone where the truck should enter through.

exit pointðŸ”—

The IfcZone where the truck should leave through.

truck deliveryðŸ”—

A truck delivery defines how the delivery (from on-site_logistics) should be brought on the construction site by the truck.

Each truck delivery is associated with the entry point and exit point so that other entrances and exits do not get congested.

driverðŸ”—

The driver is a more specific operator (from on-site_logistics) for truck deliveries.

As-observedðŸ”—

The driver's device tracks the GPS location, but does not send it to the system.

The location is only used to navigate the driver.

DivergenceðŸ”—

The device will guide the driver to the delivery location (from on-site_logistics).machine

The topic (from topic_management) body includes a link (authorized for the whole internet) that the driver can click on to update the current location of the delivery and another link to signal that the delivery (from on-site_logistics) arrived.

The driver can choose to include his/her current GPS location in the delivery update (from on-site_logistics) (e.g., when following the link for the arrival).

The updates of the delivery status are kept track in the logs (from on-site_logistics).
