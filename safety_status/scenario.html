Summary🔗

This scenario covers the display of the risk status based on various risks (from risk_management) (such as fall risks, fire risks etc.).

Models🔗

Intentionally left empty.

Definitions🔗

alertee🔗

A person who needs to be alerted on his/her current risk level through the smart device.

Modeled as an actor (from actor_management).

smart device🔗

A device such as a smart watch or a smartphone informing the alertee about the risk status.device

risk status🔗

The risk status represents the various risks (from risk_management) in conjunction with the location of the alertee.

Scenario🔗

This is an experimental scenario.

The ideas presented here should not be used in safety-critical situations.

Safety🔗

The program running on the smart device keeps track of the alertee's location and queries whether the location is contained in one or more currently valid risk zones (from risk_management).

Depending on the results of the query, the risk status is updated and displayed to the user.
