Truck guidance

Dag Fjeld Edvardsen <dag.fjeld.edvardsen@catenda.no>, Marko Ristin <rist@zhaw.ch>

Summary

This scenario concerns truck drivers arriving at the construction site to deliver cargo.

Models

ðŸ”—plan/main

The as-planned BIM model provided after the planning phase and updated throughout the construction.

It is going to be updated as the building grows.

This is the federated model of all the individual domain models (core is Site model, Structural model and Electrical model).

ðŸ”—observed/main

This model contains the real time delivery status.

ðŸ”—archived/observations

This keeps track of accomplished deliveries.

Definitions

ðŸ”—planner role

Planner role is for all people who are allowed to change a delivery.

ðŸ”—delivery

Delivery is an IfcTask, which has a text, a ScheduleStart and ScheduleFinish (both IfcTaskTime. It can be associated with a location IfcZone. The cargo is a text property of the delivery ("10 windows, 2 doors").

The delivery is assigned to a driver, a entry point and exit point and a contact person.

ðŸ”—contact person

The person who should be contacted if the driver have questions.

ðŸ”—pickup person

The person responsible for picking up the cargo.

ðŸ”—entry point

The IfcZone where the truck should enter through.

ðŸ”—exit point

The IfcZone where the truck should leave through.

ðŸ”—driver

The driver is an IfcActor.

ðŸ”—delivery status

Consideration for architecture: we need to introduce a custom class to represent the delivery status (label and depending on the label, last_location and last_observed_time).

ðŸ”—notifiee

The notifiee is an IfcActor that is notified when the delivery status is updated.

Scenario

As-planned

The planner role creates and updates the deliveries.

As-observed

The driver's device tracks the gps location, but does not send it to the system.

Divergence

The device will guide the driver to the location on the site where the delivery should take place. The driver updates the status of the delivery.

The notifiees are notified when the status of the delivery is updated.

Analytics

The KPI for delivery-in-time is updated, both delivery in number per week and variance. This is based on date from archived/observations.

The planner role decides under which status to archive the delivery (for KPI).

Scheduling

Notify the notifiees if the delivery is not ready when the pickup-time has passed.

Safety

Intentionally left empty.
